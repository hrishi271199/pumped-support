Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a workout?

Tap the Home tab, choose a template or start a blank workout, then log your sets, reps, and weight. PUMPED automatically detects personal records as you go.

What is PUMPED Pro?

PUMPED Pro unlocks the social feed, progress analytics, friends, and leaderboards. You can subscribe monthly or yearly from the Profile tab.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Profile and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I add friends?

Open the Friends tab and tap the add button in the top right. Search for your friend's username and send them a request.

Can I create custom exercises?

Yes. When adding an exercise to a workout, tap "Create Custom Exercise" at the bottom of the search list. You can also manage them from your Profile.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. When you're signed in, your workouts and PRs sync to the cloud automatically. Sign in on a new device and your data will be there.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions. Find PUMPED Pro and tap Cancel.
